USABILITY - TEXAS DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION

PROBLEM

The Texas Department of Transportation (TxDoT) wants to create a digital media newsroom.

I needed to find out if creating a newsroom was viable or not for TxDoT. If yes, then how do we pursue it ?

If the newsroom is not viable, what are the other ways in which TxDot can reach its goal ?

PROCESS

I followed the entire user research process. The steps were:

1. Client Kickoff

2. Background Research

3. Competitive Evaluation

4. Surveys

5. Interviews

6. Actionable Insights

CLIENT KICKOFF

The Client Kickoff is the first part of the user research process.

It helped me understand the goals of the client

I created an affinity diagram with the purpose of understanding which over-arching questions had to be tackled to help the client reach their goals.

BACKGROUND RESEARCH

The Background research involved tackling the over-arching questions from the affinity diagram.

The questions I tackled during my research involved:

1. How do people consume information today ?

2. How does visual content influence the consumption of information ?

3. How does personalisation influence the consumption of information ?

COMPETITIVE EVALUATION

The Competitive Evaluation involves finding how organisations in similar or dissimilar sectors are solving the same problem .

I performed competitive evaluation by comparing newsrooms across various sectors and using several factors.


The categories of newsrooms were:-

University newsrooms

Government Institutional newsrooms

Established Private Brand newsrooms

Independent Digital newsrooms


The factors over which the newsrooms were compared, included:-

Visual Design

Content Personalization

Brand Strength

Discoverability of newsroom

Content Engagement

Image Presence

Video Presence

Page views

Reach
