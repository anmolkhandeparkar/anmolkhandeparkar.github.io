USABILITY - TEXAS DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION

PROBLEM

The Texas Department of Transportation (TxDoT) wants to create a digital media newsroom.

I needed to find out if creating a newsroom was viable or not for TxDoT. If yes, then how do we pursue it ?

If the newsroom is not viable, what are the other ways in which TxDot can reach its goal ?

PROCESS

I followed the entire user research process. The steps were:

1. Client Kickoff

2. Background Research

3. Competitive Evaluation

4. Surveys

5. Interviews

6. Actionable Insights

CLIENT KICKOFF

PROCESS

AlphaLearn AR was created using the Unity 3D Game Engine and the Vuforia SDK. It was programmed in the C# language.

The project has been configured to work with Android Phones.

The user points the camera to a page of a particular book and a 3-D model of the alphabet appears on the page of that book.

For example, if you point the camera to a page filled with pictures of an ant and an apple, it will show a 3-D model of the letter A.

The project is currently built to work with just one particular book for alphabets.
